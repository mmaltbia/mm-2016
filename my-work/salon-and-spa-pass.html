Branding & Web Development for Salon & Spa Pass

PHP, WordPress, Email Campaign, HTML5, CSS3, Photoshop, Illustrator

Salon & Spa Pass is a monthly subscription for beauty services in New York City. Members chose from varying credit amounts to use as payment at partner salons.

The Challenge

Start up Salon & Spa Pass desired branding and a website that would help them acquire early stage salon partners as well as members.

The Solution

I developed branding, imagery, and a website that was tailored to the specific services offered by Salon & Spa Pass partners. User feedback was obtained and integrated into the site with a focus on clarity of messaging.

The Result

The website turned out to be fresh and inviting, with copy that clearly explains the benefits of subscription. It was designed so that the Salon & Spa Pass team can update their website as the company grows, without it ever looking neglected.

We also included additional functionality like a scheduling system and a salon location search.

All of this adds up to a beautiful, interactive onboarding experience for potential partners and subscribers.
